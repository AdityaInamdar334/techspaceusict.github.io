Techspace USICT

Best place to nurture self.

The purpose of the club is to bring the technology and science community in USICT Campus together into one cohesive club. The Club will work within USICT and reach out to various courses and classes of USICT. The main functions of the club are to encourage academic growth along with technical skills.


Let's Join Hands

About us

Techspace is the “of the students, by the students, for the students” technical society of USICT that works towards providing students with immense opportunities in the field of technology. We aim to provide an environment where, students can tinker with ideas and experiment and capitalize on the power of a strong community
Our vision is to:

  • To inculcate the culture of sharing knowledge and values
  • To be the best place to nurture oneself
