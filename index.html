About Us

Techspace is the “of the students, by the students, for the students” technical society of USICT that works towards providing students with opportunities to grow outside . We aim to provide an environment where, students can tinker with ideas and experiment and capitalize on the power of a strong community
Our vision is to:

  • To inculcate the culture of sharing knowledge and values
  • To be the best place to nurture oneself
